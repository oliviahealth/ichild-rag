signs you might be pregnant a missed period This is a common sign of early pregnancy if it has been more than a week since you expected your period you might be pregnant however this is not a sure sign as some women have an irregular period and tender breasts hormonal changes that come with pregnancy may cause sore and swollen breasts early on nausea and vomiting you may know this as morning sickness which is nausea that occurs due to pregnancy though it is called morning sickness it can occur at any time of the day frequent urination pregnancy causes the amount of fluid in your body to increase resulting in more frequent urination the best way to know if you are pregnant is to take a pregnancy test
