SPOTTING

Spotting is the presence of a

small amount of light pink, red,
or brown discharge. This can

occur when ovulating or may be
an early sign of pregnancy. If
you have spotting at the time

when you are supposed to have
your period, you may want to

take a pregnancy test.

WHEN TO BE CONCERNED

See your provider if you have discharge that is

yellow, green, clumpy, or has a foul odor. If you are
having itching or burning sensations, discomfort, a
rash, or any sores associated with abnormal

discharge, see your doctor.
