STEPS TO GIVE YOURSELF AN INSULIN
INJECTION

1.) Clean the site with alcohol.

2.) Push the needle at a 90 degree angle into
the site (ensure the needle is all the way in).
3.) Push plunger down at steady rate and pull
needle out.

WHERE CAN | INJECT THE INSULIN S
DURING PREGNANCY?

The abdomen is the best spot during pregnancy. It will get in

the bloodstream faster. Ensure the injection is 2 inches away
from the belly button.

The fleshy, outer surface of your arm or the top, outer part

of your leg can also be used if the abdomen can't.
