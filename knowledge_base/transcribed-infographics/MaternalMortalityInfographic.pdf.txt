Maternal Mortality

Maternal mortality refers to death during
pregnancy and pregnancy-related deaths up to 42
days after delivery. This is a pressing issue
globally, but especially in the United States.

Causes of Maternal Death

e Cardiovascular conditions
e Infection

e Severe bleeding

e Heart disease

e Blood clots

e Hypertension

e Stroke

Mental Health Can Play A Role

Women suffering from postpartum depression or
postpartum anxiety are at higher risk of maternal
death due to mental health issues. Some common
causes are substance use disorders and suicide.
Screening for postpartum depression and anxiety
should be performed in the weeks after birth.
